# AI-Mind Finger Drawing（腦心畫）V1.3

A bilingual, mobile-friendly local browser experience for creating mood-inspired abstract artwork from a short finger drawing.

## V1.3 updates

- Adds a compact **5-second / 10-second drawing choice**.
- Keeps 5 seconds as the quick daily experience and adds 10 seconds for users who want more lines and detail.
- Updates Chinese and English descriptions to state clearly that 5 and 10 seconds are **creative interaction choices**, not scientifically validated psychological measurement durations.
- Describes the result as **mood-inspired art for creative expression and self-reflection**, not a psychological, medical, or health assessment or diagnosis.
- Normalizes line-density calculations by the selected duration so the 10-second option is not automatically treated as more energetic simply because it lasts longer.
- Preserves V1.2 mobile page locking, native mobile save/share, compact desktop workspace, local privacy mode, and bilingual interface.

## Privacy and interpretation boundary

Drawing and analysis occur locally in the browser. No drawing data is uploaded, stored, or sent to third parties. Results are generated from line features for creative expression and self-reflection only; they are not psychological, medical, or health assessments or diagnoses.
